Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 March 2026

Credit Split ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance tool that helps two people sharing a credit card reconcile their shared spending. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

DataHow We Get ItWhy We Need It
Email address and passwordYou provide at sign-upAccount authentication
Display nameYou provide at sign-upIdentify you to the other card holder
Credit card transactions (date, amount, description, card member name)CSV upload or Plaid bank connectionCalculate who owes what
Payment attributionsYou assign in the appTrack which payments cover which transactions
Plaid connection data (institution name, card last-4 digits)Plaid APIDisplay connected bank info and sync transactions

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Bank account numbers or sort codes
  • Full credit card numbers
  • Bank login credentials (handled entirely by Plaid — we never see them)
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Analytics, tracking, or advertising data

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Credit Split service — calculating and displaying shared credit card balances between you and the other card holder. We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share your data with third parties (other than Plaid for transaction retrieval)
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Use your data to build profiles or make financial product recommendations
  • Aggregate your data with other users' data

4. Plaid

If you choose to connect your bank account, we use Plaid to retrieve your transaction history. When you connect via Plaid:

  • You authenticate directly with your bank through Plaid's secure interface — we never see your bank login details
  • Plaid sends us transaction data only (dates, amounts, descriptions, card identifiers)
  • We store a Plaid access token on our server to fetch updated transactions — this token cannot be used to move money or access your bank login
  • You can disconnect your bank at any time from Settings, which deletes the access token

Plaid's own privacy policy is available at plaid.com/legal.

5. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in Supabase Cloud (hosted on AWS). Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and all communication is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+). Access to your data is protected by Row-Level Security — only you and the other person on your shared account can see it.

6. Data Retention

  • Transactions and attributions — kept until you clear your data or delete your account
  • Plaid connection — kept until you disconnect your bank
  • Account data — kept until you delete your account
  • Invite codes — expire and are deleted after 7 days

7. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • View all your data in the app (Dashboard, Transactions screens)
  • Clear all transaction data and attributions (Settings → Clear All Data)
  • Disconnect your bank and delete the Plaid connection (Settings → Connected Banks)
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently (Settings → Delete Account)

Under GDPR, you also have the right to request a copy of your data or ask us to erase it. Contact us at the email below.

8. Children

Credit Split is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
